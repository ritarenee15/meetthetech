A lot! Let’s talk about some basics.
String – a collection of characters in a specific sequence
Can be made with since or double quotes
“The cow jumped over the moon”
‘Hi Mom!’
If you want to put single quotes within your string, you can
either:
“I tapped my sister and said ‘You’re it! ‘”
‘I tapped my sister and said \’You’re it!\’’
